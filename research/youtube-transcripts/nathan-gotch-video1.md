# Nathan Gotch – AI SEO Content Production Strategy (Transcript Notes)

In this session, Nathan Gotch explains how modern SEO content production works using AI tools and structured keyword targeting.

Key strategy explained:

SEO content should always begin with keyword intent analysis.

Instead of writing random blog posts:

1. Identify keywords with business value
2. Group related keywords into topic clusters
3. Build authority around one main topic

Nathan emphasizes topical authority as the most important ranking factor today.

Example workflow:

Step 1:
Choose a primary keyword target

Example:
“AI SEO tools”

Step 2:
Create supporting articles:

- best AI SEO tools
- AI SEO workflow
- AI content optimization strategy
- AI keyword clustering methods

Step 3:
Link articles together internally

This builds topical authority signals for Google.

He also explains AI-assisted writing workflow:

Research → Outline → Draft → Optimize → Publish

AI tools help:

- generate outlines
- speed up drafts
- improve keyword coverage
- structure headings correctly

Important warning:

AI content should never be published without editing.

Instead:

Human review improves:

clarity  
accuracy  
trust signals  

Final lesson:

Best SEO results come from combining:

AI speed + human strategy + topic clustering
